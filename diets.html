Our diet:

A desire to lose weight is a common motivation to change dietary habits, as is a desire to maintain an existing weight.

Many weight loss diets are considered by some to entail varying degrees of health risk, so many people choose to follow a 1,500-calorie diet plan to jumpstart weight loss and control their food intake.

We explains to you how to follow a 1,500-calorie diet, including foods to eat, foods to avoid and tips for healthy, long-term weight loss.

1-A One-Week Sample Meal Plan:

Here is a nutritious, one-week 1,500-calorie sample menu. The meals can be adapted to fit any dietary preference, including vegetarians and those eating gluten-free. The following meals are around 500 calories each
